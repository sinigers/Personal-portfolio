Hotel complex website Design Case Study



Problem

A hotel komplex from seven houses, mergered with competitive guest houses and hotels located in the immediate vicinity.



The Solution

To design website focusing on building a simple navigation and consistent structure of information. Clear distinction of the brand from the competitors. This would help users quickly find important information that influences their decisiont to book room.

My Roles

User research

User interface
